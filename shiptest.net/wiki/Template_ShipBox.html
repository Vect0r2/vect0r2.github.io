The hex code for the color of the box's accents.

Example usage of the template: 

{{ShipBox
 | name         = Box-class Hospital Ship
 | shortname    = Box-class
 | color        = 88cc99
 | manufacturer = Unknown
 | faction      = Independent
 | crew         = 9
 | propulsion   = Plasma Engines
 | buyable      = Yes
 | traits       = 
* Full medical bay
* Little weaponry
}}
