Ryuunosuke is a confederation of smaller enitys of the Maebta System It has a respectable population, and is considered to be a nice place to live, barring the tropical climates of it's planets. Ryuunosuke has been a member of CLIP since 400, but de facto in 372.

Notable Locations

Ryuunosuke

The capitol and name of said confederation. It is a quite nice place to live, however it's tropical climate makes it uncomfortable for outsiders.

Abandoned Nanotrasen Mining Facilities

An intresting sight is some abandoned Nanotrasen mining facilites. Maebta itself is quite resource rich, and an early Nanotrasen venture was dedicated to mining these resources. However these were abandoned during the Xenofauna war.

Culture

Ryuunosuke Culture

In a stark contast to the Kalixcian colonies around them, a lot of emphasis is placed on family and personal relationships. Additonally, spirituality is a big part of natural life, despite the urbanized nature of the planet. A common rite of passage is visiting or camping in a nature reserve. History

Colonization

Early history isn't very clear as it was never recorded in great detail. Sometime in 311, the system was colonized by a Solarian citizen, however who and when they stopped being supported by the Solarian government isn't clear. Around 355, the planet gained the attention of the URFZ who offered to give aid and protection in exchange for being apart of Zohil. With the various pirate threats at the time, Ryuunosuke accepted and very briefly became apart of Zohil. For around 20 years, anyways.

Relations

Neutral with Solgov. Tada! As a member of CLIP, naturally they have good relations with them, although many citizens are much less enthusiastic about being apart of CLIP compared to other member planets. This is not to say they would rather not be apart of CLIP, but the general sentiment of things would largely be the same without them lingers.

Playing a character from this location

As a resident of the Maebta System, you might...

  • Have a spiritual connection to nature.
  • Have a closer connection to your family compared to other spacers.
  • Care more about 'natural' family rather than found family.

Others probably...

  • Think you're a tree hugger.
  • Less 'loyal' to CLIP, and by extension, the CMM.
  • Maybe a bit *too* quiet.
