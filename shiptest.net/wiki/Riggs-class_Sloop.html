Description

The Riggs-class is Kasagi-Fischer Partnership’s mainstay in the independent ship market. Spacious, affordable, and versatile, the Riggs offers basic capabilities for everything a Frontier spacer might need in a convenient, easy-to-modify platform, and by default come equipped with a basic medbay, a small security office, atmospherics recycling and equipment to support an APLU utility exosuit. Thanks to this versatility, the Riggs has become extremely popular among moderately-wealthy independent captains, and can be found doing everything from mining to shipping to surveying Frontier planets.

The Riggs-class Sloop is a middle-of-the-road generalist vessel, with a crew size placing it on the larger end of independent vessels; this is a vessel with the capacity to do pretty much anything your mind desires.

List of standard equipment and features:

  • An HP Flaming Arrow, two HP Candors and an HP Detective Special.
  • A fully functional medical suite including surgical tools and a sleeper.
  • A fully functional APLU Ripley unit, awaiting assembly.
  • A key morale device - a Boombox.
  • Two P.A.C.M.A.N. generators and one S.U.P.E.R.P.A.C.M.A.N., alongside dueling S.M.E.S. units.
  • A (mostly) empty Starboard Maintenance Wing ripe for engineering projects.

Gameplay

  • Mining: The Riggs is equipped with an APLU, which is a versatile mining unit; alongside having a dedicated miner.
  • Engineering: An untapped Starboard wing is ripe for the picking to any interested engineer trying to test some... Interesting designs.
  • ...Whatever you want! The Riggs is equipped with a diverse crew catalog, and has enough room to facilitate any gimmick in mind!
