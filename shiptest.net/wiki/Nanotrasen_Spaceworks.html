Nanotrasen Spaceworks
Basic Information
Official Name: Nanotrasen Spaceworks
Other Names: Nanotrasen, NT, "The Company"
Headquarters: Unknown
Structure: Shipbuilder
Denonym(s): Not Specified
Founded: Unknown



Nanotrasen Spaceworks is the ship building arm of Nanotrasen. They have been involved with the design and manufacture of many ships that roam the frontier.

Ships

NT Spaceworks has created the following ships. Note that this list is non-exhaustive

  • Skipper-class Heavy Cruiser
  • Gecko-class Salvage Runner
  • Ranger-class Aid Vessel
  • Delta-class Frigate
  • Bubble-class Colonial Ship
  • Mark.II Dwayne-class Long Range Mining Transport
  • Meta-class Freighter
